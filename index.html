About Us

Our company was formed in 2003, delivering high-quality work locally in the Mabopane area and beyond. We cater for diverse needs from kitchen units to built-in cupboards.


No matter how big or small your project, we give it our full attention. Our team consists of specialists in carpentry and furniture design, so whether you are looking for furniture, a fitted kitchen or wardrobe, our team will be able to accomodate you and your unique taste.


Armed with a wealth of experience and a history of diverse projects, we are well-placed to handle both domestic and commercial projects. We also offer cut and edge services, with different boards in an assortment of colours
